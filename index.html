About Community

Paradise Cove is a 62 home community on the west side of St. George, Utah. The development was built by Ence Homes starting in 1994. Paradise Cove HOA offers homeowners full lawn maintenance, paid water and a structural home owner's insurance policy. The community also offers beautiful ponds and palm trees with mature landscaping for the enjoyment of its residents.

For the arts and entertainment enthusiast, Paradise Cove is close to downtown St. George which offers historic 19th-century buildings with charming retail shops, a library and an art museum. For those that thrive on entertainment and the arts, the Tuacahn Center for the Arts and Kayenta Art Village offer various events throughout the year.

Outdoor enthusiasts will find Paradise Cove is near many walking, running and biking trails including the Santa Clara River trail that leads to Tonaquint and Confluence Parks and Halfway Washington Trail that leads to Snow Canyon Parkway. The City of St. George hosts many running and biking events throughout the year. Snow Canyon State Park and Red Cliffs National Conversation Area are only minutes away and feature world class hiking to unique red and white rock formations, lava tubes, and arches. Zion National Park is only an hour away. Other national parks like the Grand Canyon, Bryce Canyon, Arches, Canyonlands, and Capitol Reef are within a day's road trip. St. George is also home to 11 golf courses with many being world class 18-hole championship courses.

Paradise Cove is very conveniently located minutes away from Interstate 15 that goes north to Salt Lake City and southwest to Las Vegas. Major retail outlets like Walgreens, Albertsons and Home Depot are within walking distance as well as many restaurant options. Just a few exits away off I-15 are Costco, Target, and Walmart. St. George is also home to a regional airport serving American, Delta and United airlines with non-stop service to Phoenix, Salt Lake City, and Denver.
